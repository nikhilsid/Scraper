    Oakland, CA

    8/6/2012

    I've been to this place several times and it's a real hit or miss. Several key notes:

    - This place has a HUGE used tire inventory. Probably the biggest I've seen in the Bay Area.

    - They make up prices on the spot, but I guess that's not uncommon of used tire businesses. When you pick your tires, they'll bring over one of the more senior guys who will stroke his chin for a few seconds and blurt out a number. If you catch them in a good mood, you can get a deal. Catch them in a bad mood and the price can be unrealistically high. I've experienced both extremes. It's quite frustrating when you get the latter.

    - Attitude, yes, it exists here. It's quite annoying but I think as a business in East Oakland, you probably have to have a little attitude given the patrons the business deals with regularly.

    - This place offered much better deals several years back. It's not as attractive as it once was.

    - Screen your used tires thoroughly before you buy... I've bought damaged tires here before because I just didn't notice a cut in the sidewall until I got home. Look for bubbles in the sidewall particularly.

    - It's a very rough area, but what used tire business isn't?

    If you are nearby, this place is still worth a look but I wouldn't travel for it.
