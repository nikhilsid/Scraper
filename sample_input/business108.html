A message to the Yelp Community:

Please don't be deceived by the negative reviews on Yelp, they don't represent the opinion of the vast majority of our customers. People who are happy with our services not always go online to give us praises, they show their appreciation by giving us repeat business and referrals. Yelp is a great tool that may be used in some cases to destroy the reputation of a good business.

We are honest, experienced, professional & customer service oriented. We always take the time to explain to our customers what we know about their vehicle based on our inspection and make necessary recommendations. We want the customer to have as much information as possible so they are able to make an informed decision about the repairs.  The customer has the opportunity to authorize or decline the recommended repairs prior to starting the job.

If you want to learn more about us, we encourage you to visit our web site www.parkstauto.com. Thank you for reading our message.
