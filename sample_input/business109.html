About This Business

Provided by business

Specialties

With over 70 years of combined Porsche, Volkswagen, Audi, BMW, MINI and Mercedes Benz  experience, Porboys German Automotive can care for all of your Porsche, VW, Audi, BMW, MINI and Mercedes Benz  car needs, from scheduled maintenance to complete rebuilds and restorations.  We do it all, and have been for a long time.  Give us a try!

We specialize in complete repair and restoration of all automobiles made in Germany - Porsche, VW, MINI, Audi, BMW and Mercedes Benz.  We are a State of California Licensed Smog Test Only Station. We can perform ALL smog checks on ALL cars.  No appointment necessary, just pop in!

History

Established in 1993

Back in 1970, Joe took his first ride in his Dad's Porsche 911S.  He was amazed. He knew from that day on, he would work with cars in some capacity.  Always a tinkerer, he learned quickly how to fix and make cars better and faster.  After a stint in the US Marine Corps, he was back working with Porsches - fixing, selling, restoring and best of all, driving them.  At the ripe age of 30 he decided it was time to have his own place.  Porboys was born.  Two locations and almost 16 years later, Porboys, Inc. has expanded and now includes 880 Smog Test Only.

Meet the Business Owner: Anne Z.

In 2000, Annie joined Joe and the team at Porboys as the assistant to the assistant manager.  She is our "customer service whiz" and loves to chat about your car, your kids, your pets and current events.  Please feel free to stop by any time and say hello, meet our team and be assured we will provide you with the best possible customer service.
