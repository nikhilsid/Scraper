    BEWARE! If you use your ATM to pay for gas they charge you a $.45 fee.

    For those slow at math....
    For instance you drive across town, for gas that is at 3.37 a gallon, and you get 15 gallons, it's actually 3.40 a gallon with the ATM fee.

    Also, the pumps are ridiculously slow. I concluded that they want you to give up short of a full tank because of the row of impatient looking customers waiting in line behind you. Thus, affecting the price further, had you paid with ATM.

    Not necessarily worthwhile to drive further down Park or all the way from Webster, unless you have cash or happen to be in the area.
