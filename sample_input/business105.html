    *** Total time:  75-90 min each way.  Total Cost: $45 --$36 .

Check baggage at curb and stroll on in.     I am late for everything. Hence, my Yelp headline. I don't know why. Careful calculation goes into planning my day, but I never seem to factor in time for unexpected traffic, a wardrobe malfunction, a power outage while I'm drying my hair, you name it.

    Between work and personal trips, I fly somewhere at least twice a month, and nearly every time, I arrive at the airport a jittery mess of nerves because I'm cutting it too close.

    As such, as far as airport parking goes, my resulting demand is that it be as effortless as possible. I need to work in negative time. I need a freaking miracle.

    Thanks to Yelpers' suggestions, I tried Fasttrack's Blue Lot on my last trip and couldn't believe the efficiency. First, when you arrive, they tell you where to park. Wait, what? Organization? That's crazy!

    Then - here's where it gets really exciting - the shuttle just has to swing by one row of cars to get everyone. No mind-numbing, multiple-stop ride at 5 mph here. I threw my car into park and was moving to the airport in less than two minutes. Wheeee!

    Now for some more crazy organization: the driver writes down your space on a slip of paper, and when you return from your trip, you hand that to your driver and he or she takes you directly to your car. It's so obvious, but yet no one seems to employ this system. Pure luxury!

    Best of all, the prices are very reasonable, and as other Yelpers have posted, you can find great coupons online for as low as $6 / day.

    Excellent prices, fast service, no-frills action. Love it.
